Development and implementation of real-time optimal planning and adaptive sampling strategies for multi-platform oceanographic operations in the Gulf of Mexico.

<!--more-->

## Overview

This cutting-edge research focuses on integrating ocean forecasting models with adaptive sampling strategies to optimize multi-platform oceanographic observations. The work combines real-time ocean predictions with intelligent sampling algorithms to maximize scientific return from oceanographic campaigns.

## Project Goals

- Develop real-time operational forecasting systems for the Gulf of Mexico
- Implement adaptive sampling algorithms for optimal platform deployment
- Coordinate multi-platform operations (gliders, buoys, ships, satellites)
- Improve ocean state estimation through data-driven sampling optimization

## Key Components

### Real-time Ocean Modeling
- High-resolution numerical ocean predictions
- Data assimilation for improved forecast accuracy
- Rapid model-to-operations pipeline

### Adaptive Sampling Strategies
- Optimal path planning for autonomous platforms
- Feature-targeted sampling algorithms
- Dynamic mission planning based on forecast uncertainty

### Multi-platform Coordination
- Integration of diverse observing platforms
- Communication protocols for real-time adjustments
- Collaborative sampling strategies

## Technologies & Methods

- **Ocean Models**: ROMS, HYCOM, regional high-resolution models
- **Optimization Algorithms**: Path planning, objective function design
- **Platform Technologies**: Autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs), gliders, profiling floats
- **Data Systems**: Real-time data transmission and processing

## Related Publications

- Mirabito, Haley, et al. (2025), *OCEANS 2025 - Great Lakes*, IEEE

## Applications

- **Rapid Response**: Hurricane forecasting and response operations
- **Feature Studies**: Tracking and sampling mesoscale eddies, fronts, and upwelling events
- **Process Studies**: Targeted observation of oceanographic phenomena
- **Operational Oceanography**: Support for maritime operations and safety

## Collaborators

This work involves collaboration with multiple institutions and operational centers, integrating expertise in ocean modeling, autonomous systems, and operational oceanography.
